Gyde is a Shopify app that lets you create interactive product recommendation quizzes — no coding required. Shoppers answer a few questions and land on a personalised results page showing the products that best match their needs.

Whether you sell skincare, supplements, apparel, or anything in between, Gyde helps you turn browsers into buyers by guiding them to the right product every time.

## What you can do with Gyde

- **Build quizzes in minutes** using the visual quiz builder, AI generation, or ready-made templates
- **Map products to quiz choices** so the right items surface on the results page
- **Embed quizzes anywhere** on your storefront — as a floating bubble, an inline section, or a product-page teaser
- **Show personalised recommendations** with a built-in results page and one-click Add to Cart
- **Track performance** with session analytics, completion rates, and add-to-cart attribution
