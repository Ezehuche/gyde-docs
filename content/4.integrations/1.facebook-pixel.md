Connecting a Meta (Facebook) Pixel to your quiz lets you track shopper behaviour through the quiz funnel and use that data for ad targeting and conversion optimisation.

## Set up the Pixel integration

1. Open the quiz in the Gyde app.
2. Go to **Quiz → Integrations**.
3. Click **Connect** next to **Facebook Pixel**.
4. Enter your **Pixel ID** and click **Connect with Facebook Pixel**.

To find your Pixel ID, go to [Meta Events Manager](https://business.facebook.com/events_manager) → Data sources → your pixel → Settings.

## Events tracked

Once connected, Gyde fires the following standard Meta events:

| Event | When it fires |
|---|---|
| `ViewContent` | When a shopper opens the quiz |
| `Lead` | When a shopper submits the quiz |
| `AddToCart` | When a shopper adds a product to cart from the results page |

These events use Pixel's standard event names so they integrate directly with Meta's ad attribution, Custom Audiences, and conversion tracking — no custom event configuration needed.

## Use cases

- **Retargeting** — create a Custom Audience of shoppers who opened the quiz but didn't submit, and retarget them with product ads.
- **Lookalike audiences** — use quiz completers as a seed audience to find similar shoppers.
- **Conversion optimisation** — optimise ad campaigns for `Lead` or `AddToCart` events generated by quiz traffic.

## Notes

- The Pixel script loads **only when the quiz is open** — not site-wide. It is injected by the Gyde embed script when the quiz initialises.
- If your Shopify theme already loads the same Pixel ID, the two instances coexist without conflict (Meta deduplicates events by browser session).
