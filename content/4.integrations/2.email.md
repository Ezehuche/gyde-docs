Get notified by email every time a shopper submits a quiz on your store. This is the quickest way to stay on top of new quiz completions without logging into the Gyde app.

## Set up email notifications

1. Open the quiz in the Gyde app.
2. Go to **Quiz → Integrations**.
3. Click **Connect** next to **Email Notification**.
4. Enter the email address where you want to receive notifications.
5. Click **Connect with Email Notification** to save.

You can add multiple email addresses by setting up the integration multiple times with different addresses.

## What the notification contains

Each notification email includes:

- The quiz name and submission timestamp
- A summary of the shopper's answers
- The products that were recommended on the results page
- A link to view the full submission in the Gyde app

## Use cases

- **Sales team alerts** — notify your team when a high-intent shopper completes the quiz and adds something to cart.
- **Daily digest** — forward notifications to a shared inbox for review during daily stand-ups.
- **CRM integration** — if your CRM supports email-to-lead parsing, forward Gyde notifications to it automatically.

For more advanced automation (e.g. triggering Klaviyo flows based on quiz answers), use the [Webhook](/integrations/webhook) or [Zapier](/integrations/zapier) integrations.
