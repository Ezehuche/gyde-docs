Connecting Gyde to Mailchimp lets you grow your email list and trigger post-quiz automations automatically. Every time a shopper submits a quiz, their email is added to the Mailchimp audience you choose — with their quiz answers mapped to custom merge fields if needed.

## Requirements

Your quiz must include an **email question** — Mailchimp contacts require an email address. Without one, the integration will not create contacts.

## Set up the integration

1. Open the quiz in the Gyde app.
2. Go to **Quiz → Integrations**.
3. Click **Connect** next to **Mailchimp**.

### Step 1 — Authorise with Mailchimp

Click **Login with Mailchimp** and log in to the Mailchimp account you want to use.

### Step 2 — Select an audience

Choose the Mailchimp audience (list) that quiz respondents should be added to.

### Step 3 — Map quiz fields to contact fields

Map your quiz's email question to the Mailchimp **Email** field. Optionally map name and other questions to the corresponding merge fields.

Click **Connect with Mailchimp** to activate.

## Use cases

- **Post-quiz welcome email** — set up a Mailchimp automation that sends a personalised product recommendation email immediately after a shopper joins the audience.
- **Segmented campaigns** — use Mailchimp tags or groups to segment contacts by their quiz answers and send targeted campaigns.
- **Abandoned quiz follow-up** — combine with the Webhook integration to capture partial submissions and trigger re-engagement emails.
