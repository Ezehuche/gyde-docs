Gyde supports team collaboration at the workspace level. You can invite colleagues to help build quizzes, review analytics, or manage product mappings — without sharing your Shopify login.

## How to invite team members

1. In the Gyde dashboard, click **Settings** in the left sidebar.
2. Go to the **Members** section.
3. Click **Invite member** and enter the team member's email address.
4. Click **Send** — they'll receive an email invitation to join the workspace.

You can invite multiple teammates by clicking **Add more** before sending.

## Managing access

From the Members page you can:

- **View** all current workspace members and their roles.
- **Remove** a member from the workspace (their quizzes and data remain intact).
- **Transfer ownership** to another member.

::alert{type="warning" icon="lucide:triangle-alert"}
  Removing a member does not delete their quizzes or any product mappings they created. Only the workspace owner can remove members.
::

## Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Full access — manage members, billing, and all quizzes |
| **Member** | Create and edit quizzes, view analytics, manage product mappings |
