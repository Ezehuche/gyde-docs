Gyde integrates directly with the Shopify Theme Editor through native app blocks and an app embed — no code editing required. There are three ways to surface a quiz on your storefront:

| Method | Best for |
|---|---|
| **Floating Popup** | Site-wide quiz bubble that appears on every page |
| **Inline Section** | Embedding a quiz directly on a specific page (homepage, landing page, collection page) |
| **Product Page Teaser** | A CTA card on a product page that opens a quiz pre-loaded with that product's context |

---

## Floating Popup (App Embed)

The floating popup shows a bubble in the corner of every page on your store. Shoppers click it to open the quiz in an overlay.

### Setup

1. In your Shopify admin, go to **Online Store → Themes**.
2. Click **Customize** on your active theme.
3. Open **App embeds** (the puzzle-piece icon in the left panel).
4. Find **Gyde Widget** and toggle it on.

### Settings

| Setting | Description |
|---|---|
| **Quiz handle** | Paste the handle of the quiz you want the bubble to open (e.g. `skin-type-quiz`). Leave blank to load all published popup quizzes automatically. Find the handle in **Quiz Settings → Widget → Quiz handle**. |
| **Bubble shape** | **Circle** — a round icon button. **Rectangular banner** — a pill-shaped button with text. |
| **Widget position** | Bottom-right or bottom-left corner of the screen. |
| **Bubble color** | Background colour of the bubble button. |
| **Show teaser on page load** | When on, a tooltip card appears above the bubble after a short delay to catch attention. When off, the teaser only appears when a customer adds a product to their cart. |
| **Teaser message** | The text shown in the teaser card (e.g. "Not sure which product is right for you?"). |
| **Button text** | Text on the rectangular banner style button (e.g. "Find your perfect match"). |

## Inline Quiz Section

The inline section block embeds a quiz directly on a page — the quiz renders as part of the page content, not in an overlay.

### Setup

1. In the Theme Editor, navigate to the page where you want to embed the quiz.
2. Click **Add section** and search for **Gyde Section**.
3. Select it and configure:
   - **Quiz handle** — the handle of the quiz to embed (required).
   - **Display type** — `Standard` (inline iframe), `Modal` (quiz opens in a modal when triggered), or `Full-page`.

The quiz section block works on any Shopify 2.0 theme that supports app blocks.

---

## Product Page Teaser

The product page teaser displays a visual call-to-action card on product pages. When a shopper clicks it, the quiz opens pre-loaded with the current product — so they arrive at the quiz already partway through the funnel.

### Setup

1. In the Theme Editor, open a **Product** page template.
2. Click **Add block** and search for **Gyde Product Teaser**.
3. Configure:
   - **Quiz handle** — the quiz to open when the CTA is clicked.
   - **Heading** — the teaser headline (e.g. "Not sure this is the right pick?").
   - **Description** — supporting text below the headline.
   - **Button text** — CTA label (e.g. "Take the quiz").
   - **Button color** — accent colour for the button.

---

## Finding your quiz handle

Every quiz has a unique handle automatically generated from its title. You can find and copy it from **Quiz → Settings → Widget → Quiz handle** inside the Gyde app.

---

## Legacy themes

If your theme does not support Online Store 2.0 app blocks, the Inline Section and Product Page Teaser blocks will not be available. You can still use the **Floating Popup** (App Embed), which works on all Shopify themes.

For legacy themes, you can also embed the quiz by adding a direct link or button to your theme HTML that calls `window.Gyde.open()` to trigger the popup programmatically.
