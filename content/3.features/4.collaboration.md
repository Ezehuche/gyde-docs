After a shopper submits the quiz, Gyde replaces the standard "Thank you" slide with a personalised **Product Recommendations** page — right inside the quiz iframe. No page reload or redirect needed.

## What shoppers see

The results page shows:

- A **heading** and **description** (configurable in the Thank You slide of your quiz — or default copy if left blank).
- A **product grid** with one card per recommended product, showing:
  - Product image
  - Product title
  - Price (from the first available variant)
  - **Add to Cart** button
  - **View product** link that opens the product page in the main browser tab

The product grid is responsive and adapts to the width of the embed container.

## Add to Cart

Shoppers can add a product directly from the results page without leaving the quiz. The Add to Cart flow:

1. The shopper clicks **Add to Cart** on a product card.
2. Gyde calls the Shopify storefront AJAX API (`/cart/add.js`) to add the variant.
3. The button changes to **Added ✓** in green to confirm success.
4. A `cart:updated` event is dispatched so any cart drawers or live cart counts on your theme update automatically.

Each add-to-cart event is also tagged with internal metadata (`_gyde_quiz_id` and `_gyde_session_id` as line item properties) so you can track quiz-attributed orders in your Shopify admin.

## How products are selected

Gyde picks recommendations based on the [product mappings](/features/submissions) you configured:

- For each multiple-choice or picture-choice answer the shopper selected, one product is chosen at random from that option's mapped products.
- Duplicates across choices are removed.
- The final set is displayed in the order they were selected.

If the shopper chose a specific product from the **mid-quiz product carousel** (popup mode only), that product is shown exclusively — overriding the calculated recommendations.

## Customising the results heading

The heading and description on the results page come from your quiz's **Thank You** slide:

1. Open the quiz in the builder.
2. Click the **Thank you** question slide.
3. Edit the **Title** and **Description** fields — these support basic HTML for bold, links, etc.

If the Thank You slide has no title, the results page defaults to "Top picks **for you**" with a subtitle of "Based on your answers."

## View product links

The "View product" link on each card opens the product's Shopify storefront URL in the top-level browser tab (`target="_top"`), taking the shopper to the full product page without breaking out of any iframe constraints.
