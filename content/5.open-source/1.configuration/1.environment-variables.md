The **Gyde Widget** App Embed block adds a floating quiz bubble to every page of your Shopify store. Shoppers click the bubble to open a quiz in an overlay — without ever leaving the current page.

## Enable the App Embed

1. In your Shopify admin, go to **Online Store → Themes**.
2. Click **Customize** on your active theme.
3. In the left sidebar, click the **App embeds** icon (puzzle piece).
4. Find **Gyde Widget** in the list and toggle it **on**.
5. Configure the settings (see below), then click **Save**.

The embed is active site-wide — it appears on every page including the homepage, collection pages, product pages, and the cart.

## Settings reference

### Quiz handle

Paste the **handle** of the quiz you want the bubble to open (e.g. `skin-type-quiz`).

Find your quiz handle inside the Gyde app at **Quiz → Settings → Widget → Quiz handle**.

Leave blank to let Gyde automatically load all of your published popup quizzes. If multiple quizzes are published as popup type, they will each create their own bubble.

### Bubble shape

| Option | Description |
|---|---|
| **Circle** | A round icon button — clean and unobtrusive |
| **Rectangular banner** | A wider pill-shaped button with text — more prominent |

### Widget position

Choose which corner the bubble anchors to: **bottom-right** (default) or **bottom-left**.

### Bubble colour

Sets the background fill of the bubble button. Defaults to `#6366f1` (indigo). Use your brand's primary colour for consistency.

### Show teaser on page load

When **on**, a small tooltip card appears above the bubble a few seconds after the page loads — drawing attention to the quiz.

When **off**, the teaser only appears when a shopper adds a product to their cart — a more contextual trigger that catches shoppers at a decision-making moment.

### Teaser message

The text shown inside the teaser tooltip (e.g. `"Not sure which product is right for you?"`). Keep it short — one sentence works best.

### Button text

Only applies to the **rectangular banner** shape. This is the label shown on the pill-shaped button (e.g. `"Find your perfect match"`).

## How the popup works

When a shopper clicks the bubble:

1. A slide-in panel opens from the side of the screen.
2. The quiz loads inside the panel.
3. As the shopper answers each question, a **product carousel** briefly appears showing the products mapped to that choice — giving instant visual feedback.
4. After submission, the panel shows the **results page** with personalised product recommendations and Add to Cart buttons.
5. The panel can be closed at any time with the × button.

## Troubleshooting

**The bubble isn't showing up**
- Check that the App Embed is toggled on and saved in the Theme Editor.
- Confirm that at least one quiz is published with its display type set to **Popup**.
- If you entered a quiz handle, verify it matches exactly (case-sensitive, hyphens not spaces).

**The bubble appears on mobile but not desktop (or vice versa)**
- The bubble renders at all viewport sizes. If it's hidden, a CSS rule from your theme may be overriding its `z-index` or `display` property. Check your theme's CSS for conflicts.
