The **Gyde Product Teaser** block adds a small call-to-action card directly on product pages. When a shopper clicks it, the quiz opens pre-loaded with the current product's context — letting them quickly confirm whether that product is the right fit, or discover a better alternative.

## Requirements

- Your theme must support **Online Store 2.0** app blocks.
- The teaser block is added at the **product template** level, not as a standalone section.

## Add the Teaser block

1. In your Shopify admin, go to **Online Store → Themes → Customize**.
2. Open a **Product** page template (e.g. the Default product template).
3. In the left panel, click **Add block** inside the product information section.
4. Search for **Gyde Product Teaser** and select it.
5. Configure the settings and click **Save**.

## Settings reference

### Quiz handle

The handle of the quiz to open when the shopper clicks the CTA (e.g. `skin-type-quiz`). Find it in **Quiz → Settings → Widget → Quiz handle**.

### Heading

The headline shown on the teaser card. Default: `"Not sure this is the right pick?"` — but customise it to match your brand voice.

### Description

Supporting text below the headline. Keep it to one short sentence explaining the benefit of taking the quiz.

### Button text

The label on the CTA button (e.g. `"Take the quiz"`, `"Find my match"`, `"Get a recommendation"`).

### Button colour

Accent colour for the button background. Defaults to your quiz's primary colour.

## How the teaser flow works

When a shopper clicks the teaser button on a product page:

1. The quiz opens in a popup overlay.
2. The quiz is pre-configured to show the current product as a mid-quiz product option — so the shopper can quickly confirm or redirect.
3. After submitting, the results page shows a personalised recommendation that either confirms the current product or suggests a better alternative.
4. The shopper can add to cart directly from the results page without navigating away from the product page.

This flow is particularly effective for products where shoppers often feel uncertain (supplements, skincare actives, technical equipment) — it converts hesitation into a guided decision.

## Positioning the teaser

By default, the teaser card appears as a block within the product information column. You can drag it above or below other blocks (price, quantity selector, Buy button) in the Theme Editor to control its position.

For maximum impact, place it **directly below the Add to Cart button** so it catches shoppers who are pausing before committing.
