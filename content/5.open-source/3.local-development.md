## Quiz widget not appearing

**Symptom**: The floating bubble or inline section is not visible on the storefront.

**Checks**:
1. Confirm the App Embed is toggled **on** and the Theme Editor changes were **saved**.
2. Confirm the quiz is **published** (unpublished quizzes are never shown to shoppers).
3. If you specified a quiz handle in the App Embed settings, verify it matches the handle in **Quiz → Settings → Widget → Quiz handle** exactly (lowercase, hyphens).
4. Check that your theme supports OS 2.0 if you're using the Section or Product Teaser blocks.

---

## Products not showing on the results page

**Symptom**: After completing the quiz, the results page says "Check back soon for personalised product recommendations" even though products are mapped.

**Checks**:
1. Open the quiz builder and go to the **Products** tab — confirm that at least one choice has products mapped to it.
2. Confirm the shopper actually selected a choice that has mapped products (choices with no mappings produce no recommendations).
3. Check that the mapped products are still available in your Shopify catalogue (deleted or archived products are excluded).

---

## Add to Cart button not working

**Symptom**: Tapping "Add to Cart" on the results page has no effect.

**Checks**:
1. The quiz must be embedded on a Shopify storefront domain (`.myshopify.com` or your custom domain). The Add to Cart button calls Shopify's AJAX API (`/cart/add.js`), which only works on the storefront.
2. Ensure the product variant is **available for sale** and not sold out.
3. Check your browser console for CORS or 422 errors from `/cart/add.js`. A 422 typically means the variant ID is invalid.

---

## Mid-quiz product carousel not appearing

**Symptom**: No carousel appears between questions even though products are mapped to the choices.

**Check**: The mid-quiz carousel only shows when the quiz **display type is Popup**. It does not appear in Standard inline or Modal embeds. Verify the quiz's display type in **Quiz → Settings → Widget → Display type**.

---

## The teaser tooltip shows even when I don't want it

**Symptom**: The teaser card pops up on every page immediately.

**Fix**: In the Theme Editor, open the **Gyde Widget** App Embed settings and turn off **Show teaser on page load**. The teaser will then only appear when a shopper adds a product to their cart.

---

## Inline section not available in Theme Editor

**Symptom**: The "Gyde Section" block doesn't appear when searching for it in the Theme Editor.

**Cause**: Your theme does not support Online Store 2.0 app blocks.

**Fix**: See [Theme Compatibility](/open-source/self-hosting) for your options on legacy themes.

---

## Quiz handle not found

**Symptom**: The floating popup shows an error or nothing loads.

**Fix**: Double-check the handle you entered in the App Embed settings matches the one shown in the Gyde app. Handles are auto-generated from the quiz title and look like `skin-type-quiz` — lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens only. If you renamed the quiz, the handle may have changed; copy it fresh from **Quiz → Settings → Widget → Quiz handle**.

---

## Getting help

If you've checked the above and the issue persists, contact Gyde support and include:

- Your Shopify store URL
- The quiz handle or quiz ID
- A description of the issue and any browser console errors
