## Create a new quiz

From the Gyde dashboard, click **New Quiz**. You'll be presented with three ways to get started:

- **Generate with AI** — describe your product category and goals; Gyde drafts a complete quiz for you in seconds.
- **Start from a template** — pick a pre-built quiz tailored to common use-cases (skincare finder, supplement recommender, style quiz, and more).
- **Start from scratch** — open a blank quiz and build it question by question.

Whichever path you choose, you'll land in the **Quiz Builder**.

## The Quiz Builder

The quiz builder works like a slide editor. Each question occupies its own screen, and shoppers move through them one at a time — keeping the experience focused and engaging.

### Adding questions

Click the **+** button to insert a new question slide. Supported question types include:

| Type | Best for |
|---|---|
| **Multiple choice** | Product preference questions — the backbone of most recommendation quizzes |
| **Picture choice** | Visual questions where shoppers pick by image |
| **Short text** | Open-ended follow-up questions (name, email, concerns) |
| **Statement** | Intro or transition slides with no input required |
| **Thank you** | The final slide — shown after the quiz is submitted |

### Customising a question

Click any question slide to edit its text. Then use the **Options Panel** on the right to:

- Mark the question as **Required** (shoppers must answer before advancing)
- **Insert an image** to illustrate the question
- Set up **Conditional logic** to branch to different questions based on answers (see [Conditional Logic](/features/form-settings))

### Reordering questions

Drag a question card up or down in the left panel to reorder it. Use the three-dot menu on any question to **Duplicate** or **Delete** it.

## Renaming and managing quizzes

To rename a quiz, go to the dashboard, click the three-dot menu on the quiz card, and choose **Rename**.

To delete a quiz, click **Delete** from the same menu. Deleted quizzes are moved to the Trash and can be restored within 30 days.

## The quiz handle

Every quiz has a **handle** — a short, URL-friendly identifier automatically generated from the quiz title (e.g. `skin-type-quiz`). The handle is used to:

- Pin a specific quiz to the floating popup bubble in your Shopify theme settings.
- Reference the quiz in [Quiz Section blocks](/open-source/configuration/authentication) on product or landing pages.

Find and copy the handle from **Quiz Settings → Widget → Quiz handle**.

## Publishing your quiz

Quizzes save automatically as you build. When you're ready to make the quiz live, click **Publish** in the top-right corner. Unpublished quizzes are never shown to shoppers.

After publishing, [map products to quiz choices](/quickstart/how-to-delete-forms) so the right items surface on the results page.
