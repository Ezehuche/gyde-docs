Product mapping is what turns your quiz into a recommendation engine. When a shopper picks a choice, Gyde records which products are mapped to it. After submission, the results page shows a combined set of products from all the choices the shopper selected.

## Open the Products tab

In the quiz builder, click the **Products** tab (the fourth tab in the top bar). This opens the product mapping panel alongside your quiz questions.

## Map products to a choice

1. Click a **multiple-choice or picture-choice question** in the left panel.
2. The Products tab shows each choice option for that question.
3. Click **Add products** next to any choice and search your Shopify catalogue by title.
4. Select one or more products to link to that choice.

Repeat for every choice on every question that should influence recommendations.

## How recommendations are calculated

When a shopper submits the quiz:

1. Gyde looks at every choice they selected.
2. For each choice that has mapped products, one product is picked at random from that choice's product list.
3. The selected products are deduplicated and displayed on the results page.

This means a shopper who selects three choices will see up to three recommended products — one representative pick per choice — keeping the results page clean and focused.

## Mid-quiz product carousel (popup quizzes only)

When a quiz is displayed as a **popup**, Gyde shows a brief product carousel after a choice is made. The shopper can:

- **Browse** the products mapped to that choice.
- **Select one** — if they do, only that product will appear on the final results page.
- **Skip** — the quiz continues and normal recommendation logic applies at the end.

## Tips

- Map **multiple products** to a single choice to give the algorithm variety and improve personalisation for different shopper profiles.
- Use **conditional logic** alongside product mapping to route shoppers with specific combinations of answers to more targeted choices.
- Keep each choice focused — avoid mapping unrelated products to the same option.
