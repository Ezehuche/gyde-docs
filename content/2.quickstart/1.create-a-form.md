## Install from the Shopify App Store

1. Open the [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com) and search for **Gyde**.
2. Click **Add app** and follow the OAuth prompt to grant the required permissions.
3. Once installed, Shopify will redirect you back to the Gyde app inside your Shopify admin.

> **Permissions requested**: Gyde requests read/write access to products, customers, orders, themes, and storefront content. These are needed to fetch your product catalogue, embed the quiz widget on your theme, and create embed pages.

## First login

When you open Gyde for the first time, a **workspace** is automatically created for your store — you don't need to set anything up manually.

Your workspace is where all your quizzes, product mappings, and analytics live. You can invite team members to collaborate from the **Settings** page.
